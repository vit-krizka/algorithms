SIEVE OF ERATOSTHENES

The Sieve of Eratosthenes is one of the oldest-known algorithms for finding all prime numbers up to any given limit! The algorithm is attributed to Eratosthenes, a Greek mathemetician born in the third century BCE.

THE KNAPSACK PROBLEM

Imagine that you’re a thief breaking into a house. There are so many valuables to steal - diamonds, gold, jewelry, and more! But remember, you’re just one person who can only carry so much. Each item has a weight and value, and your goal is to maximize the total value of items while remaining within the weight limit of your knapsack. This is called the knapsack problem.
